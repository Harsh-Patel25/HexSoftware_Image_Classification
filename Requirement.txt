IMAGE_CLASSIFICATION_PROJECT


Dataset Source:
The dataset used in this project is derived from the Microsoft Cats vs Dogs dataset,
publicly available on Kaggle:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shaunthesheep/microsoft-catsvsdogs-dataset

This dataset is commonly used for educational and research purposes in
computer vision and deep learning tasks.

--------------------------------------------------

Project Structure: It is the structure of the Image Classification project.

Image_Classification_Project/
├── dataset/
│   ├── train/
│   │   ├── cat/      -> Training images of cats
│   │   └── dog/      -> Training images of dogs
│   │
│   └── test/
│       ├── cat/      -> Testing images of cats
│       └── dog/      -> Testing images of dogs
│
├── cat_dog_model.keras   -> Trained deep learning model
